Company Background
Built in Kinston. Trusted Across Eastern NC.

Central Heating and Air Conditioning of Kinston, Inc. has been a full-service HVAC and mechanical contractor since July 1977. What started as a local service company has grown into one of Eastern North Carolina's most experienced mechanical contracting firms.

We specialize in commercial, industrial, and residential HVAC — from small-scale service calls to major mechanical construction projects. Our in-house automated sheet metal fabrication shop gives us a competitive edge that most contractors in the region can't match.

  • Fully licensed and insured mechanical contractor
  • Complete automated sheet metal fabrication on-site
  • Serving Eastern North Carolina since 1977
  • Commercial, industrial, and residential capabilities
  • Experienced in school, healthcare, and government bid projects
